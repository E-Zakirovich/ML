Hi this file is going to explain all about dataset

This dataset is collection of houses at the USA. With this db, u can get the prices of hoses. There is 7 columns, They are:
    1 Avg. Area Income - Average yearly income of people living in that neighborhood.
    2 Avg. Area House Age - Average age (in years) of houses in that area.
    3 Avg. Area Number of Rooms - Average number of rooms per house in that area.
    4 Avg. Area Number of Bedrooms - Average number of bedrooms per house.
    5 Area Population - Number of people living in that area.
    6 Price - House price
    7 Address - Text address of the house.

That's it.
